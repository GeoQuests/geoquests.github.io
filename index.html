GeoQuests

GeoQuests is a new real life, non-virtual
multiplayer scavenger hunt and geocaching game.
Because we know that playing with your friends online is
fun, but playing with them in person is better.

Gameplay

Play anywhere in the world.

Because all quests are created by users, for users, you may be invited to quests spanning your campus, city or even country! If you haven't been invited to any quests in your area, you can always create on and invite your friends!

Race against your friends.

GeoQuest is a fun and social game, but first and foremost, it's a race. Play against other invitees and your friends in the quest to reach the final destination. Whoever gets there first wins, it's that simple.

Work with others on the quest.

Throughout the quest work with your friends or the other participants to figure out clues and brag about success, of course. However, remember that in the end it's a race, so don't give too much away to the competition!

Use anything at your disposal.

If you're playing with solely friends, the creator of the quest may have created personalized clues, so you'll have to use any information on them you can find. If you're playing with strangers, the clues are going to be more objective so don't be afraid to look things up!

Tips for Quest Making

Writing good clues for a quest.

When writing your first and all clues for a quest keep in mind who your target audience is. If you plan to create a game in your area where anyone can play, keep your clues objective and factual. If you're playing with friends, you can use personal information to see who knows you the best!

Know the location of your quest.

Keep in mind the location of your quest while creating one. It is easy to get "pin happy" and create a lot of clues and add many locations. If you're playing in an urban area with public transportation, you can possibly spread out your clues a bit more. Otherwise, you may want to keep the locations more centrailzed, while making the clues a bit harder to solve.

Provide incentive to your friends.

Obviously it's fun to play with your friends in a racing environment, however, providing incentive to your friends is a great way to up the participation rate. It's also a great way to add fun to a treasure hunt type GeoQuest. If you're planning a birthday hunt, try also adding rewards at each checkpoint, for extra fun!
